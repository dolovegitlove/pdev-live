The No-BS Guide

Think of PDev like building a business the smart way - you don't just start coding. You figure out what's already out there, what's missing, and then build something better.

  1. /idea or /eval - What are you building? New concept or improving existing?/idea "fitness app for busy people"
  2. /bmrk - What's the competition doing? Who's winning and why?/bmrk industry "fitness apps"
  3. /gapa - What's missing in the market? What are people complaining about?/gapa industry "fitness tech"
  4. /innov - Now that you know what exists and what's missing, how can you do it 10x better?/innov product myfitapp
  5. /caps - Can you actually build this? What team/tech do you need?/caps tech myfitapp
  6. /spec - Write the blueprint. Every feature, every screen, every API./spec product myfitapp
  7. /bmrk design - What should it look like? Research the best designs in your category./bmrk design myfitapp
  8. /sop - Break it into tasks. Phase 1, Phase 2, etc. What gets built first?/sop product myfitapp
  9. /pv - Validate everything. Make sure nothing's missing before you start coding./pv myfitapp
